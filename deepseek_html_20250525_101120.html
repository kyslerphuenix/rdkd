Frequently Asked Questions

Is this method safe to use?

Yes, when used properly. The c3c-fbstate utility runs locally on your device and doesn't send your data anywhere. However, you should never share your fbstate with others as it contains sensitive login information.

Why do I need Kiwi Browser?

Kiwi Browser is one of the few Android browsers that supports Chrome extensions. This allows you to run the c3c-fbstate utility directly on your mobile device without needing a computer.

How long does the fbstate last?

Your fbstate typically remains valid until you log out of Facebook or after about 60 days of inactivity. You'll need to generate a new one when it expires.

Can I use this on iPhone?

Currently this method only works on Android devices because iOS doesn't support browsers with extension capabilities like Kiwi Browser. For iPhone users, you'll need to use a computer.

What if I get an error during installation?

Make sure you've enabled Developer Mode in Kiwi Browser before trying to install the extension. Also ensure you're selecting the complete downloaded ZIP file, not just its contents.
